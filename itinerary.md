## THURSDAY – APRIL 2, 2026

### 🇯🇵 Tokyo → Osaka (Bullet Train Day)

**Morning – Asakusa → Shinkansen Station**

* Check out from Sunbright Asakusa 101
* Taxi from Asakusa to **Tokyo Station** *or* **Shinagawa Station**

  * Time: ~20–30 minutes
  * Cost: ~¥2,500–3,500
  * (Taxi recommended with luggage)

**Mid-day – Shinkansen (Bullet Train)**

* **Train:** Nozomi (fastest, most frequent)
* **Route:** Tokyo / Shinagawa → Shin-Osaka
* **Travel time:** ~2 hr 30 min
* **Seats:** Reserved seats (sit together)
* **Cost (adult):** ~¥14,720 one-way
* **Kids (6–11):** ~50% fare
* Large luggage: reserve oversized luggage seats if needed

**Arrival – Osaka**

* Arrive **Shin-Osaka Station**
* Transfer to Nihonbashi / Namba area:

  * Taxi: ~20–25 minutes (~¥2,500–3,000)
  * OR subway (Midosuji Line → Sennichimae Line)

**Afternoon**

* Arrive at Osaka Airbnb
* **Airbnb check-in**
* Short rest

**Evening**

* Walk to Kuromon Market or Dotonbori
* Casual Osaka street food dinner

**Overnight:** Osaka (Airbnb – Nihonbashi / Namba)

**Accommodation:**

* Osaka Airbnb – Nihonbashi / Namba area
* Booking: https://www.airbnb.com/l/3VwVOqsS
