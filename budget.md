## Budget Notes

- **Convenience store meals** (7-Eleven, Lawson, FamilyMart) are shockingly good in Japan. A full meal for one person costs ¥500–800 (~$3–5). Great option for breakfasts or when kids are tired.
- **Vending machines** everywhere: drinks ¥130–200 (~$1). Cheaper than cafés.
- **Kuromon Market** prices are tourist-premium. Budget ¥800–1,500 per person for sampling.
- **Night markets in Taiwan** are the best value meals of the trip. Budget NT$100–200 ($3–6) per dish.
- The budget above is **moderate** — you can spend less by eating at convenience stores and small ramen shops, or more by doing sit-down restaurants.
